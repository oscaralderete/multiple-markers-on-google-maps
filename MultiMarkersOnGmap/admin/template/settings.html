Multi Markers On Gmap

Instructions

a. Enter your Google maps API key (important to avoid that ugly modal notification that says something like: 'For development purposes only').

b. Enter your desired map height in pixels (map width is 100%).

c. Define your markers.

d. Put the next shortcode:

[MultiMarkersOnGmap]
wherever you want to display your map. The footer is the natural choice but for sure you can found an awesome place to locate it.
